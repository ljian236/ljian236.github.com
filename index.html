Research

I am interested in software testing, vulnerabilities mining and exploit and secure software systems.

My current work includes testing and debugging of large-scale software programs (see ICSE 2015, ICST 2015, ACM TODEAS 2015) and concurrent errors detection (see FSE 2016). I am also working on secuirty vulnerabilties analysis and exploiting for Android systems.
